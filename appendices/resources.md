### Essential Reading
1. **"Spring Boot: Up and Running"** by Mark Heckler (2021)
   - Modern, practical approach
   - Covers Spring Boot 2.x+ concepts

   - Comprehensive Spring Framework coverage
   - Great for understanding foundations

3. **"Learning Spring Boot 3.0"** by Greg L. Turnquist
   - Covers modern Spring Boot concepts
   - Good transition to 4.x
